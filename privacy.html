Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Work Hours Calculator UK ("the App"), developed by SoftSplit d.o.o., handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

Work Hours Calculator UK does not collect any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device and does not transmit any information to external servers.

Data Storage

Any data you enter into the App is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Is not transmitted to any server or third party
  • Is not accessible to SoftSplit d.o.o. or anyone else

Advertising

Work Hours Calculator UK uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases to remove advertisements and unlock premium features. Purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or disclose any personal information to third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is safe for use by all ages.
