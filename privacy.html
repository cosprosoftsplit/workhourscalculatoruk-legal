Privacy Policy

WorkHoursCalculatorUK — Last updated 2026-04-09

Introduction

WorkHoursCalculatorUK is developed by Cosmic Production d.o.o., based in Split, Croatia. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data with us. All data you enter stays on your device (or in your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled).

Third-Party Services

This app may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — for app distribution and in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies.
  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad serving. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

We do not operate our own analytics. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or device information beyond what Apple provides through App Store Connect analytics (which is anonymised and aggregated).

The free version does display advertising through Google AdMob (see “Third-Party Services” above). With your consent — requested via Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt — AdMob may use your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve personalised ads. If you decline, ads are still shown but are not personalised. You can change this choice at any time in iOS Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data. Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to request. If you have concerns, contact us.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
