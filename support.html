Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate my work hours?

Enter your shift start and end times, and the app will automatically calculate your total work hours, overtime, and breaks. You can track daily, weekly, and monthly totals.

Does the app support UK overtime rules?

Yes. The app is designed with UK working time regulations in mind, including standard 48-hour weekly limits and overtime calculations.

Can I export my work hours?

Yes. You can export your tracked hours for your own records or to share with your employer.

The App is not working properly. What should I do?

Try the following steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the App installed
  • Restart the App
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the App

If the issue persists, please contact us at ivan@cosmicproduction.hr with a description of the problem.

How do I delete my data?

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it within the App or by uninstalling the App entirely.
